# Data Dictionary

## reservation_id

Unique reservation identifier.

---

## guest_id

Unique guest identifier.

---

## guest_name

Guest full name.

---

## hotel_name

Property name.

Possible values:

- Alecrim ao Chiado
- Casa Palmela
- Alfama Yellow House

---

## email

Guest email address.

---

## email_is_valid

Indicates whether the email passed validation rules.

Values:

- true
- false

---

## guest_age

Calculated guest age.

---

## age_segment

Guest age classification.

Values:

- young_adult
- adult
- mature_adult
- senior
- unknown

---

## stay_pattern

Stay duration classification.

Values:

- short_stay
- weekly_stay
- extended_stay

---

## is_weekend_traveler

Indicates whether the reservation includes weekend travel.

Values:

- true
- false

---

## is_high_value_stay

Indicates above-average reservation value.

Values:

- true
- false
