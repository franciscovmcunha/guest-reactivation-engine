# Business Case

## Problem

Hotels continuously acquire new guests through OTA channels and direct bookings.

After checkout, most guests never receive structured follow-up communication.

As a result:

- Direct booking opportunities are lost
- OTA dependency remains high
- Customer retention remains low
- Guest lifetime value is underutilized

---

## Solution

Create a centralized guest intelligence platform capable of:

- Identifying contactable guests
- Segmenting customers
- Supporting reactivation campaigns
- Enabling future loyalty initiatives

---

## Expected Outcomes

### Commercial

- More direct bookings
- Higher guest retention
- Increased customer lifetime value

### Operational

- Better customer data quality
- Improved guest profiling
- Enhanced marketing efficiency

### Strategic

- Reduced OTA dependency
- Stronger customer relationships
- Foundation for loyalty programs
