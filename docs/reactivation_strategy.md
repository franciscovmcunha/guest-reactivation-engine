# Guest Reactivation Strategy

## Vision

Transform historical hotel guests into repeat customers through structured reactivation initiatives.

---

## Phase 1

Email-Based Reactivation

Target:

- Historical guests
- Contactable guests
- Completed stays

Benefits:

- Low operational cost
- Scalable communication
- Direct booking opportunities

---

## Phase 2

Guest Segmentation

Segments include:

- High-value guests
- Families
- Weekend travelers
- Returning guests
- Age-based segments

---

## Phase 3

Loyalty Program

Potential components:

- Discount vouchers
- Reward points
- Experience credits

---

## Phase 4

Experience Integration

Potential integration with hospitality experiences and partner services.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Build a hospitality customer intelligence ecosystem focused on:

- Retention
- Loyalty
- Direct Revenue Growth
- Guest Experience
