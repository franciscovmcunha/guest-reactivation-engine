# Gold Layer

## Overview

The Gold Layer contains business-ready marts designed for hospitality analytics and guest reactivation use cases.

All marts are optimized for reporting, dashboarding and campaign execution.

---

## CRM

### mart_contactable_guests

Validated guest database containing contactable guests.

### mart_reactivation_candidates

Guests eligible for reactivation campaigns.

---

## Segmentation

### mart_guest_age_analysis

Age-based customer segmentation.

### mart_family_travel

Family travel analysis.

### mart_weekend_travelers

Weekend travel analysis.

---

## Reporting

### mart_contactable_distribution

Executive KPI measuring guest contactability rates by hotel.
